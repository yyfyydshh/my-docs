# Open Source vs Cloud Scraping

> Compare self-hosted open source scrapers with managed cloud scraping platforms across cost, control, maintenance, and reliability.

## Open Source Scraping

Tools like Scrapy, Puppeteer, Playwright, and Selenium give you full control.

**Pros:**
- Full control over every component
- No per-request cost after infrastructure setup
- Flexibility to customize any aspect
- No vendor lock-in

**Cons:**
- You manage all infrastructure
- Anti-bot detection is your responsibility
- Maintenance burden falls on your team
- Scaling requires more engineering effort

## Cloud Scraping Platforms

Managed platforms like Octoparse, ParseHub, and ScrapingBee handle infrastructure and anti-bot complexity.

**Pros:**
- No infrastructure to manage
- Built-in proxy rotation and fingerprint management
- Faster time to data
- Less technical expertise required

**Cons:**
- Per-request or subscription cost
- Less flexibility for non-standard targets
- Vendor dependency
- Less visibility into how data is collected

## Comparison

| Factor | Open Source | Cloud Platform |
|---|---|---|
| Cost model | Upfront infrastructure | Per-request or subscription |
| Setup time | Days to weeks | Minutes to hours |
| Maintenance | High (your team) | Low (provider handles) |
| Anti-bot resilience | Your responsibility | Built-in |
| Flexibility | Maximum | Limited to platform capabilities |
| Scaling | Engineering effort | Platform handles |
| Best for | Custom, large-scale projects | Teams without engineering resources |

## When to Choose Which

- **Open source** if: You have engineering resources, need full control, or have non-standard targets
- **Cloud platform** if: You need data quickly, have limited technical resources, or want minimal maintenance burden
