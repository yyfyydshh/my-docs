# What is AI Web Scraping?

> AI web scraping uses AI to detect page structure, generate matching rules, and extract data from raw HTML - more resilient than rule-based scrapers.

## Traditional vs. AI Scraping

**Traditional scraping** requires manual page inspection, writing XPath/CSS selectors, testing selectors, and monitoring updates.

**AI scraping** shifts this burden to a model: describe what you want in natural language, the AI detects structure and generates rules, the AI adapts to minor page changes.

## Benefits

- **Faster setup** - Get data in minutes instead of hours
- **More resilient** - AI adapts to layout changes
- **Less technical knowledge required** - Natural language instead of XPath
- **Handles complex pages** - Multi-column layouts, conditional content

## Limitations

- **Cost** - AI inference adds per-page cost
- **Edge cases** - Unusual layouts may still require human validation
- **Speed** - Slower than optimized traditional selectors for large batches
