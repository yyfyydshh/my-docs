# How to Scrape Walmart

> How to extract product listings, prices, reviews, ratings, availability, and seller data from Walmart for competitive intelligence and market research.

## What to Collect

| Data Type | Fields |
|---|---|
| Product listings | Name, price, original price, rating, review count, availability |
| Product details | Description, specifications, brand, item number, images |
| Reviews | Rating, title, text, date, verified purchase flag |
| Seller data | Seller name (for third-party marketplace items) |

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start from search or category pages">
    Collect broad product listings from search result or category pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract listing data">
    Gather name, price, rating, review count, and availability from search results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit product detail pages">
    Extract full descriptions, specifications, shipping options, and seller information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle pagination">
    Collect all relevant results across search result pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Use Cases

- **Price monitoring** - Track Walmart prices against competitors
- **Assortment analysis** - Monitor what products Walmart carries in a category
- **Review monitoring** - Track review sentiment and volume over time
- **Inventory tracking** - Monitor stock availability patterns
