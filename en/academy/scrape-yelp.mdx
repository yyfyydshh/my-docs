# How to Scrape Yelp

> How to extract Yelp business listings, profiles, reviews, ratings, categories, and contact signals for local research and review analysis.

## What to Collect

| Data Type | Fields |
|---|---|
| Business listings | Name, address, phone, category, rating, review count, price range |
| Reviews | Rating, text, date, reviewer name, useful/funny/cool votes |

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search by category and location">
    Start from search result pages for your target category and geography.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect listing data">
    Extract business name, rating, review count, address, and phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit business profile pages">
    Extract hours, attributes, photos, website URL, and full review data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle pagination">
    Yelp search results paginate; collect all relevant listings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
