# How to Scrape Google Maps

> How to extract local business listings from Google Maps: search inputs, listing fields, place details, reviews, and lead enrichment.

## What to Collect

| Data Type | Fields |
|---|---|
| Business listings | Name, address, phone, website, category, rating, review count, hours |
| Reviews | Rating, text, date, reviewer name |

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define search parameters">
    Use keyword + location combinations: "dentists in Austin TX"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect listing data">
    Extract name, rating, review count, address, phone, website from search results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle map splitting">
    For large areas, divide into smaller regions and scrape each separately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract place details">
    Use Place ID to get full address, website, photos, and reviews.
  </Step>
</Steps>
