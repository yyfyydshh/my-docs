# Scraping Behind Login Walls

> How scrapers handle authenticated pages with cookies, session reuse, browser-based login, MFA limits, and account-safe operating practices.

## Approaches

### 1. Cookie and Session Reuse
Log in once, export cookies, reuse them in your scraper.

### 2. Browser Automation Login
Use Playwright or Puppeteer to automate the login flow, handle MFA, then extract session cookies.

### 3. API Authentication
OAuth 2.0 flows, API key or token-based auth. Most reliable and sanctioned by the platform.

## Best Practices

- **Use dedicated accounts** - Do not use personal accounts for scraping
- **Rotate sessions** - Do not reuse the same session indefinitely
- **Handle MFA carefully** - Automated MFA is unreliable
- **Monitor for account issues** - Watch for captchas or login alerts
- **Respect ToS** - Logging in against ToS may void your account
