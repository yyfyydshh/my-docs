# How to Scrape LinkedIn

> What is technically and operationally involved in collecting public LinkedIn profiles, company pages, jobs, and posts, with legal and ethical safeguards.

## What Can Be Collected

| Data Type | Fields | Complexity |
|---|---|---|
| Company pages | Name, industry, size, location | Moderate |
| Job postings | Title, company, location, salary | Moderate |
| Public profiles | Name, headline, location, current role | High |

## Legal Considerations

<Warning>
  LinkedIn has aggressive anti-scraping enforcement and has pursued legal action against scrapers. Consider official APIs first and consult legal counsel for commercial use cases.
</Warning>

## Alternatives to Scraping

1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** - Official paid tool
2. **LinkedIn API** - Official API with limited access
3. **Third-party data providers** - Apollo, ZoomInfo with licensing agreements
