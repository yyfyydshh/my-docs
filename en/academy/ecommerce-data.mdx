# E-Commerce Data Collection

> How teams collect product catalogs, prices, reviews, ratings, seller data, and inventory signals from e-commerce sites and marketplaces.

## What to Collect

| Data Type | Use Case | Typical Fields |
|---|---|---|
| Product listings | Category and assortment analysis | Title, price, rating, review count, images, URL, category |
| Product details | Deep product intelligence | Description, specs, variants, seller, fulfillment type, BSR |
| Reviews | Sentiment analysis and QA | Rating, title, text, date, verified purchase, helpful votes |

## Workflow Patterns

<Steps>
  <Step title="Collect listing data">
    Start with category and search result pages to build a broad product catalog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit detail pages">
    For products that matter, extract deeper fields from product detail pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor over time">
    Re-scrape key products on a schedule to track price changes and stock status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common E-Commerce Targets

- **Amazon** - Product catalog, prices, reviews, BSR tracking
- **eBay** - Product listings, seller performance
- **Walmart** - Price monitoring, product availability
- **Shopify stores** - Individual brand monitoring
